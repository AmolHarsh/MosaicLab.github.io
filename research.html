Research

The MOSAIC Lab focuses on advancing the field of ubiquitous health sensing through innovative approaches. Key research areas include:

  • **Mobile Health Sensing**: Development of wearable devices to monitor physiological and behavioral signals.
  • **Acoustic and Electromagnetic Sensing**: Utilizing physical signals from the human body for real-time analysis.
  • **Behavioral Mapping**: Bridging the gap between raw signals and meaningful health insights.
